Exploratory testing

About

  • Exploratory testing is a process of simultaneous learning and testing, adapting to new information as it's encountered. Test cases are created and evolved as you explore a system, rather than fully defined first.
  • It combines heuristics, best practices, and the tester's own experience to examine the system. It's is an excellent match for accessibility as it values and uses the expertise acquired through your lived or professional experience.
